# World Cup Country Trivia

Study guides and quizzes for the four World Cup countries: France, Germany, Italy, and Spain.

## How to use this site

Each country has two pages:

- **Study Guide** — read this first. Geography, history, government, arts & culture, food, and sports. Each section has links to short videos and articles to go deeper.
- **Quiz & Answers** — test yourself. Click any question to reveal the answer and a study tip.

## The prize rule

Score **8 out of 10** on any quiz set to earn the prize. There are 3 sets per country — 30 questions total for each.
